DnDice is the most elegant dice roller for Dungeons & Dragons, Pathfinder, and any tabletop RPG. Beautifully designed, fast to use, and completely offline.

🎲 ALL THE DICE YOU NEED
Roll any combination of the standard RPG dice set:
• D4 — the caltrop
• D6 — the classic
• D8 — the octahedron
• D10 — the pentagonal trapezohedron
• D12 — the dodecahedron
• D20 — the legendary icosahedron
• D100 — the percentile dice

⚡ ROLL MULTIPLE DICE AT ONCE
Add multiple dice of any type to your pool — 2d6, 3d8, 1d20+5 — and roll them all with a single tap.

🎯 MODIFIER SUPPORT
Add positive or negative modifiers to any roll. Perfect for ability checks, attack rolls, and saving throws.

📜 ROLL HISTORY
View your recent rolls so you never lose track of what just happened at the table.

✨ SATISFYING ANIMATIONS
Each roll triggers a smooth, satisfying animation. Rolling feels good.

🌙☀ DARK & LIGHT MODE
A deep dark mode for dimly lit game rooms, and a clean light mode for daytime sessions. Your preference is saved automatically.

✅ 100% OFFLINE
No internet required. Roll dice anywhere — at the table, on the go, or in a dungeon with no signal.

PERFECT FOR:
• D&D 5e, Pathfinder, Call of Cthulhu, and any other TTRPG
• Dungeon Masters and players alike
• Board gamers who need a quick dice replacement
• Anyone who loves satisfying dice rolls

No ads. No permissions. No internet. Just dice.
